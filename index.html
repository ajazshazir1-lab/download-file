ABOUT A.G.MOOSA & CO.

Established in 1964 by (Late) Mr. Haji Muhammad Ilyas, A.G. Moosa & Co. has grown from a small wholesale setup into one of Karachi’s most trusted pharmaceutical distribution companies. With over five decades of commitment to integrity, quality, and service excellence, we now serve 4,000+ pharmacies and 200+ hospitals across the region.

Our team combines deep industry experience with innovative logistics and tech-enabled order systems to ensure timely delivery of essential medicines and FMCG products.
