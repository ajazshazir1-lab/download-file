About Us

Delivering trusted medicines since 1964

Our Story

A.G. Moosa & Co. was established in 1964 by the visionary Mr. Haji Muhammad Ilyas (Late), who had a clear mission: to deliver life-saving medicines with trust and efficiency. What began as a small wholesale operation serving a limited circle of residents in Karachi has now transformed into a dynamic and robust pharmaceutical distribution company.

Our Legacy

For over five decades, A.G. Moosa & Co. has built a legacy rooted in dedication, ethical practices, and a customer-first approach. The founders' family continues the mission with integrity and excellence.

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

To ensure the reliable delivery of pharmaceutical products across Pakistan by maintaining high standards of service, ethics, and professionalism.

Vision

To become the most trusted and innovative pharmaceutical distribution company in the country.

Values

Integrity, Reliability, Innovation, Commitment, Collaboration.
